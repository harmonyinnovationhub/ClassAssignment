LIFE IN WEEKS

This function tells us how many days, weeks and months we have left if we live until 90 years

it takes current age as the input and console.logs a message with our time left in this format:

You have x days, y weeks, and z months left.

Where x, y and z are replaced with the actual calculated numbers.

for this challenge, there are 365 days in year, 52 weesk in a year and 12 months in a year
